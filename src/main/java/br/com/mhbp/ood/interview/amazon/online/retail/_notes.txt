


We have four main Actors in our system:

Admin:

Mainly responsible for account management and adding or modifying new product categories.

Guest:

All guests can search the catalog, add/remove items to the shopping cart,
as well as become registered members.

Member:

Members can perform all the activities that guests can, in addition to which,
they can place orders and add new products to sell.

System:

Mainly responsible for sending notifications for orders and shipping updates.
